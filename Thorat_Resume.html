Resume

Work Experience:

Attending the SAP Partner Summit was one of the most memorable experiences for me. During this, I was able to hear from industry experts from 14 of SAP's Fortune 500 customers and had the opportunity of networking with executives from companies like Infosys and Microsoft. The main reason this field trip stands out to me is because I was able to hear from the top executives of SAP and their journey to where they are now. This Q+A session was inspiring, as it shows students like me that they can reach where they want to be with perseverance.

Another field trip I had the opportunity of going on was visiting the San Jose State and Santa Clara univiersities. This field trip allowed me to get a first-hand experience of the environment there and the great engineering programs.

In February, I got the chance to attend the SheLeads Conference and meet many influential women in diverse engineering fields.

Getting to hear Laura Keigelmeyer as a guest speaker was an enjoyable experience. It was inspiring to hear about how her family immigrated to the U.S and the challenges she overcame (i.e being discouraged from attending grad. school). Even with these, she was able to succeed and found work at Lawrence Livermore National Labarotory. It was interesting to hear about how she helped found the NIF automated optics program and the details of her work using the laser facility and showed me a new field I might like to explore in the future.

Two guest speakers that were influential to me this year were Kana Mishra and Aileen Inguiez. Kana Mishra, a DHS alumni, studies data science at UC Berkeley. It was interesting to hear about how she meshed her passions for data science and computer science and her work with natural language processing and data cleaning. Aileen Inguiez had a similar interest in data science and talked about her journey from college to working with Chevron. Aileen also talked about the importance of data science as the future progresses, which I thought was interesting!

Through the Engineering Academy, I had the amazing opportunity of being able to participate in the Mentor Program. As a part of this program, I've been able to meet multiple engineers in diverse fields and gotten to learn about different pathways that I wouldnt've considered before. Moreover, this program introduced me to the many different aspects of college and helped me better prepare!
